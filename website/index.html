About

Abstract

We present Neuroprobe, a benchmark for understanding how the brain processes information across multiple tasks. Neuroprobe analyzes intracranial recordings during naturalistic stimuli using techniques from modern natural language processing. By probing neural responses across many tasks simultaneously, Neuroprobe aims to reveal the functional organization of the brain and relationships between different cognitive processes.
